About Elegance Steps

Crafting Excellence Since 2015

Based in the heart of Mulund, Mumbai, Elegance Steps was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves footwear that makes her feel confident, comfortable, and sophisticated.

Our journey began with a small workshop and a big dream - to create premium footwear that combines traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibilities.

Our Values

Quality First

We source only the finest materials and work with skilled artisans to ensure every pair meets our exacting standards.

Comfort Always

Beautiful shoes should never compromise on comfort. Every design is tested for all-day wearability.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable manufacturing practices that respect our environment.

Mumbai Pride

Proudly based in Mumbai, we understand the unique needs of urban Indian women and design accordingly.

Sizing Tips:

  • Measure your feet in the evening when they're at their largest
  • Always measure both feet and choose the size for your larger foot
  • Leave a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the shoe
  • Consider the shoe style - some styles run larger or smaller
